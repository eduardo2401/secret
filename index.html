Victoria's Secret is an American company that designs lingerie and other feminine beauty products. The company was founded in San Francisco, California, in 1967 by Roy Raymond who sold it in 1982 to the corporation "The Limited Inc." and is now owned by its successor, the Limited Brands company. Since the brand was transferred, the company has been based in New York. Its first owner was Roy Raymond, although he committed suicide in 1993 by jumping off the Golden Gate Bridge.

In the year 2005, the company made sales profits of more than $ 2.4 billion through its 900 stores in the United States. Victoria's Secret products are also available online: Victoria's Secret Direct, with sales of approximately $ 870 million. The company gained notoriety in the 1990s after it will start hiring famous models for its ad campaigns and fashion shows.

Every year, twenty to forty models from the fashion world are selected to walk in the catwalk. In a typical year, this includes around half a dozen women under a contract with the company, also known as "Victoria's Secret Angels," who help spread the event, featuring the brand's underwear, music. from various very famous artists such as: Katy Perry (2010), Fall Out Boy (2013), Black Eyed Peas (2009), Ellie Gouldin (2015) and the great scenery that is produced each year with great precision to suit the different themes that are performed on the catwalk.
